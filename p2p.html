Vendor Application

Our network strictly limits visibility to trusted local providers. To get listed, complete the concierge process below.

STEP 1: Pay the Listing Fee

Send 0.05 XMR to the network address. Save your Transaction ID (TxID) as proof of payment.

STEP 2: Message Us on Session

Contact the network admin via Session Messenger.

STEP 3: Provide Your Details

In your Session message, include the following so we can publish your listing:

  • TxID (Proof of Fee)
  • Vendor Name & Bio
  • Asset & Amount (e.g., 5 XMR)
  • Price/Rate
  • Location (e.g., New Rochelle Area)
  • Your Direct Session ID

Listings are manually verified and deployed within 24 hours.
